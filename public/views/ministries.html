Minisitries

Kids & Nursery

Loving nursery workers provide a clean and safe environment for your little ones from newborn to 4 years old as you enjoy a dynamic worship service or a Life Connections class. Every child, from kindergarten to grade 5, will experience a class where they will learn fun songs, memorize Bible verses, and apply Bible truths.

Youth Group

At Bible Baptist, we strive to direct teenagers in discovering and following God's will by helping them develop a personal relationship with God, to ocnnect with other teenagers in a God-honoring atmosphere of acceptance, and to reach out to others with the life-changing truths of God's Word.

Life Connections

Our Life Connections Bible classes are a place where you can connect with friends, learn about the Bible, and get involved. There's a class and a seat waiting for you! Please stay fo donuts & coffee after the 9:30 AM service, and we can help you to a small group class that is right for you.
